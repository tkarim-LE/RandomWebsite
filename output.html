Investment Focus

What we look for

We seek passionate founders building enduring software businesses with strong fundamentals and massive market potential.

B2B Software

Enterprise and SMB software companies with recurring revenue models that deliver measurable value to their customers.

Proven Traction

Companies with $10M–$100M+ in ARR demonstrating strong unit economics, healthy retention, and efficient growth.

Large Markets

Businesses operating in expansive or rapidly growing markets with significant room for long-term value creation.

Exceptional Teams

Visionary founders and management teams with deep domain expertise, strong culture, and ambitious growth plans.

Capital Efficient

Companies that have built impressive businesses with disciplined capital allocation and a clear path to profitability.

Defensible Moats

Products with high switching costs, strong network effects, or deep technical differentiation that protects long-term value.
